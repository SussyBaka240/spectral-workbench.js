Spectral WorkBench by Public Lab (DEMO)

SpectralWorkbench.org is a web based application to collect, archive, share, and analyze spectral data. With it, you can connect your USB Desktop Spectrometry Kit, scan samples, or try the application by uploading an image or using your webcam.
