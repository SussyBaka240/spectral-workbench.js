Spectral WorkBench



What is Spectral Workbench? SpectralWorkbench.org (now inactive) is a web based application to collect, archive, share, and analyze spectral data, for Public Lab DIY spectrometers and other spectrometers. With it, you can: connect your USB Desktop Spectrometry Kit. scan and save samples. Try out the application with upload or capture image. This is a modified fork of spectral-workbench.js featuring an upload function.
